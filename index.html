Welcome to my webpage!

I am a PhD student at the Center for Climatic Research and the Department of Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences, University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am advised by Prof. Zhengyu Liu, working on the paleoclimate simulation. We are especially interested in the isotope-enabled simulation of the climate evolution and abrupt climate change events since the Last Glacial Maximum (LGM, around 21, 000 years ago).
